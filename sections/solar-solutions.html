Off Grid

Off-grid solar systems are designed for locations without access to the electrical grid. These systems provide self-sufficiency, using solar panels, batteries, and inverters to generate and store power.

On Grid

On-grid solar systems are connected to the local power grid, allowing you to send excess energy back to the utility. This system is ideal for homeowners looking to reduce energy costs without full self-sufficiency.

Hybrid

Hybrid solar systems combine both on-grid and off-grid capabilities. They allow you to store excess energy in batteries while still being connected to the grid for backup power and energy trading.
